UPCOMING GAMES

December 2020

Spirit of the North

December 4th

Spirit of the North is a breathtaking 3rd person adventure game set in the stunning frozen landscapes of Iceland. Prepare to be immersed within ancient nordic folklore in the quest to find the guardian of the northern lights. Trailblazing in new design it features little to no dialogue or narration leaving the player to take in the surroundings whilst solving, interpreting and looking for clues. We can't wait to start exploring!

Empire of Sin

1st December

Empire of sin is a strategy that promises plenty of adventure, set in the roaring 20's the player finds themself right in the heart of prohibition-era chicago, as a member of the ruthless criminal underbelly of organised crime. Left to your own devices it's up to you to work your way up and survive in this often dangerous world. However we do know one things for sure, this is a game we don't wanna miss!
